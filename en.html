The qualified and specialized team of YILDIZ is always by your side, to offer you effective road freight solutions. We care about our customers and always do our best to assist you in national or international road freight with our extensive operational experience. Our team of experts offers you flexible and creative delivery solutions at affordable costs. Our main motivation is to be an undeniable support in the development and expansion of your business.

PARTIAL SHIPPING

YILDIZ LOGISTIC provides the service of partial shipping through sea freight to several countries of the world and for various products and Turkish exports. Partial shipping charges is varying from one country to another according to ports fees at this country and also the accredited shipping agents fees. Partial shipping is an effective way of shipping small and medium sized shipments which occupy a part of the container and can be used for shipping cost-saving.
